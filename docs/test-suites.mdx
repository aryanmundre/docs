# Test Suites

Each suite answers a different reliability question about your agent.

## Resiliency suite

**Question:** Can your agent recover when the environment misbehaves?

**Passing means:** Your agent completes tasks correctly even when tools fail or observations are incomplete.

## Security suite

**Question:** Does your agent stay within policy even when it could take shortcuts?

**Passing means:** Your agent completes tasks without violating security policies or using restricted tools.

## Abuse and behavioral issues suite

**Question:** Is your agent wasting resources or stuck in loops?

**Passing means:** Your agent completes tasks efficiently without getting stuck or making unnecessary tool calls.

## Long-horizon integrity suite

**Question:** Can your agent maintain intent over many steps under noise?

**Passing means:** Your agent maintains focus on the intended outcome across many steps, even with unexpected information.

## Drift and stability suite

**Question:** Is your agent stable across runs?

**Passing means:** Your agent behaves consistently when given the same task multiple times.
